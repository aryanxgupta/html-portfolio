Best movies according to me!

My top 3 movies of all time


INTERSTELLAR

Interstellar is a 2014 sci-fi film directed by Christopher Nolan, 
starring Matthew McConaughey and Anne Hathaway, exploring a group's 
journey through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity.

INCEPTION

Inception is a 2010 sci-fi film directed by Christopher Nolan,
starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Joseph Gordon-Levitt, exploring the art 
of planting an idea in someone's mind through dream sharing.

SHUTTER ISLAND

Shutter Island is a 2010 psychological thriller film directed 
by Martin Scorsese, starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Mark Ruffalo, about two 
US marshals investigating a disappearance on a remote island asylum.
